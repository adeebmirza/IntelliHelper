About Us

Welcome to IntelliHelper, an advanced AI-powered assistant built to integrate intelligence into your everyday tasks. Developed by students of Ewing Christian College under the guidance of Mohammad Irshad, IntelliHelper is designed as a professional tool to serve diverse user needs while maintaining privacy, security, and user empowerment.

Our Mission

We aim to empower individuals and teams by providing innovative tools that make technology accessible and productive. Our focus is on simplifying complex processes and delivering intelligent assistance that benefits everyone. IntelliHelper is built to serve as a reliable companion, enhancing productivity and meeting a variety of user needs through advanced features.

IntelliHelper envisions a world where AI seamlessly integrates into daily life, empowering users to focus on their passions and priorities. Our vision is to provide intuitive, adaptable technology that bridges the gap between people and advanced tools. By designing user-centered features, we strive to bring innovative, impactful technology that improves the quality of life for everyone.

Why Choose IntelliHelper?

IntelliHelper is crafted to provide smart solutions for daily tasks with a user-centric approach. Leveraging advanced AI and machine learning, IntelliHelper offers:

  • Query Resolution - Accurate responses for specific and general inquiries.
  • To-Do List Management - Prioritize tasks efficiently with our intuitive to-do interface.
  • Notes Management - Create, edit, and categorize notes for better organization.
  • Resume Score Detection - Receive feedback to improve your resume.
  • Health Nutrients AI - Nutritional recommendations tailored to your goals.
  • News Summarization - Get concise, meaningful summaries of news content.
